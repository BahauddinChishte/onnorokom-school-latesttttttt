## Ways to Contribute

### 1. Submit Resources
- Suggest new educational resources
- Share quality video lectures
- Recommend study materials

### 2. Content Improvement
- Report errors in existing content
- Suggest better explanations
- Help improve documentation

### 3. Spread the Word
- Share EduHub with other students
- Write about your experience
- Help us reach more learners

## Contribution Guidelines

1. Ensure resources are free and accessible
2. Verify content accuracy
3. Follow our formatting standards
4. Include proper citations
5. Respect copyright laws
