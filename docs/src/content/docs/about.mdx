## Our Mission

EduHub was created with a simple yet powerful mission: to make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background. We believe that education is a fundamental right, not a privilege.

## What We Offer

- **Free Resources**: All educational content is completely free
- **Curated Content**: Carefully selected resources from trusted sources
- **Structured Learning**: Well-organized chapters and topics
- **Multiple Subjects**: Comprehensive coverage across different subjects

## Who We Are

We are a team of educators and students passionate about democratizing education. Our platform is maintained by volunteers who believe in the power of free education to transform lives.

## Get Involved

Want to contribute? Here's how you can help:
- Submit new educational resources
- Report errors or suggest improvements
- Share with other students
- Provide feedback on existing content
